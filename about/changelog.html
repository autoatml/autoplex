Changelog#

v0.2.0#

Previously, autoplex supported DFT labelling of structures primarily via VASP.

In this release, we introduce a new CASTEP interface, developed based on the ase.calculators.castep module. This addition enables CASTEP to be used directly within the autoplex workflow, laying the groundwork for high-throughput DFT workflows, such as Random Structure Searching (RSS).

üîÑ Other Changes#
